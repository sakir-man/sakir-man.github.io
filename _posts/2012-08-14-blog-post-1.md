## Research Roadmap: Crescent Visibility & Islamic Astronomy
<h2 style="text-align: justify;" data-start="396" data-end="427"><strong data-start="399" data-end="425">Key Research Questions:</strong></h2>
<h3 style="text-align: justify;" data-start="428" data-end="493">Research Roadmap: Crescent Visibility &amp; Islamic Astronomy</h3>
<p style="text-align: justify;" data-start="494" data-end="546"><strong data-start="494" data-end="546">Focus: Twilight (Syafaq) and Crescent Visibility</strong></p>
<hr data-start="548" data-end="551" />
<h3 style="text-align: justify;" data-start="553" data-end="612"><strong data-start="557" data-end="612">A. Fundamental Astronomical &amp; Atmospheric Questions</strong></h3>
<ol style="text-align: justify;" data-start="613" data-end="1212">
<li data-start="613" data-end="752">
<p data-start="616" data-end="752">How does twilight sky brightness (syafaq) affect the minimum detectable contrast between the crescent Moon and the background sky?</p>
</li>
<li data-start="753" data-end="906">
<p data-start="756" data-end="906">What are the dominant atmospheric parameters (aerosols, humidity, air mass) influencing crescent visibility during twilight in tropical regions?</p>
</li>
<li data-start="907" data-end="1058">
<p data-start="910" data-end="1058">How do geometric parameters (elongation, altitude, phase, and lunar age) interact with twilight brightness in determining crescent visibility?</p>
</li>
<li data-start="1059" data-end="1212">
<p data-start="1062" data-end="1212">To what extent do existing classical and modern visibility models adequately represent twilight conditions in low-latitude (tropical) atmospheres?</p>
</li>
</ol>
<hr data-start="1214" data-end="1217" />
<h3 style="text-align: justify;" data-start="1219" data-end="1265"><strong data-start="1223" data-end="1265">B. Observational &amp; Empirical Questions</strong></h3>

<li data-start="1266" data-end="1402">

</li>
<li data-start="1403" data-end="1534">
<p data-start="1406" data-end="1534">How consistent are naked-eye, optical-aided (binocular/CCD), and imaging-based detections of the crescent during twilight?</p>
</li>
<li data-start="1535" data-end="1669">
<p data-start="1538" data-end="1669">What discrepancies exist between predicted visibility and actual observational outcomes under different twilight intensities?</p>
</li>
<li data-start="1670" data-end="1783">
<p data-start="1673" data-end="1783">How can citizen science observations be standardized to reliably capture twilight-related visibility data?</p>
</li>
</ol>
